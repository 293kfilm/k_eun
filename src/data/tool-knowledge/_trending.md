# PART 1 — 2026 바이럴 포맷 12선
================================================================

## 1. Bodycam Comedy (경찰 바디캠 + 부조리 캐릭터)

가장 강력한 포맷 중 하나. **익숙한 다큐 형식 + 비현실적 주체** 조합.

**프롬프트 공식**
> Realistic police bodycam footage. Officer pulls over [absurd subject in
> business attire]. [Specific dialogue/action]. Documentary realism, handheld
> shaky camera, body-mounted POV, ambient radio chatter.

**예시**
- "Realistic police bodycam footage. Officer pulls over a car driven by a sentient taco in business attire."
- "Realistic police interrogation room bodycam. Officer questioning a golden retriever about a robbery."

**왜 먹히나**: 다큐 톤이 진지할수록 주체의 부조리함이 부각되어 댓글·공유 폭증.
**최적 모델**: Sora 2 (인물 디테일 강점), Veo 3 (오디오 자동 합성), MiniMax Hailuo

---

## 2. Movie Mashup (명장면 + 캐릭터 스왑)

**프롬프트 공식**
> Cinematic recreation of [iconic movie scene]. Identical lighting, framing,
> pacing, and editing. Except [character] has been replaced with [unexpected
> character]. Maintain original cinematography style.

**예시**
- "The Dark Knight interrogation scene, but Batman is replaced with SpongeBob SquarePants."
- "Godfather office scene, but the Don is a sentient blob of slime with eyes."
- "Empire Strikes Back AT-AT walker scene, but all walkers are giant rubber duckies."

**왜 먹히나**: 영화광이 즉시 인지 → 친구 태그·공유 트리거.
**최적 모델**: Sora 2, Kling 3.0 (영화 톤 재현 강점)

---

## 3. Character Role Swap (캐릭터 × 엉뚱한 직업)

**프롬프트 공식**
> [Iconic character] working as [mundane job]. [Specific work activity in
> character]. Realistic [setting]. Documentary or commercial style.

**예시**
- "Darth Vader working as a flight attendant, showing passengers safety procedures."
- "Elmo hosting a finance and investment seminar about cryptocurrency."
- "Gollum working as a retail store manager training employees."

**왜 먹히나**: 캐릭터 정체성과 상황의 미스매치가 즉각적 웃음 유발.
**최적 모델**: Sora 2 (캐릭터 일관성), Veo 3 (대사·SFX 자동)

---

## 4. Retro Recreation (시대 미학 + 현대적 트위스트)

**프롬프트 공식**
> [Decade] [format] aesthetic. Film grain, period-accurate color grading,
> [decade-specific lens look]. The [subject] is [absurd modern element].

**예시**
- "1980s toy commercial aesthetic, VHS quality, energetic announcer. The toy is a sentient hamburger that runs very fast."
- "1990s sitcom opening theme sequence, all the characters are different types of cheese."
- "1970s action movie trailer, hero is a middle-aged man who's really good at organizing closets."

**왜 먹히나**: 노스탤지어 트리거 + 현대 부조리 = 전 세대 공유.
**최적 모델**: Kling 3.0, Sora 2, Veo 3 (포맷 모방력)

---

## 5. Cameo / Self-Insert (불가능 시나리오)

Sora 2의 Cameo 기능 활용. 본인 얼굴을 사전 등록 후 시나리오에 삽입. 공유율 3–5배.

**프롬프트 공식**
> You (via cameo) are [impossible role]. [Specific absurd action]. [Setting
> with cinematic detail]. [Tone].

**예시**
- "You (via cameo) are a professional astronaut walking on Mars, doing yoga poses on the planet surface."
- "You (via cameo) are a lounge singer in a fancy Las Vegas casino, singing about tax deductions."
- "You (via cameo) are the President of the United States, every executive order is about making pizza mandatory."

**왜 먹히나**: 자기 자신이 등장 → DM·태그 폭증.
**최적 모델**: Sora 2 only (현재 Cameo는 Sora 2 전용)

---

## 6. Mundane Becomes Cinematic (일상 × 영화적 톤)

**프롬프트 공식**
> [Trivial everyday task]. Dramatic orchestral music swelling. Slow motion
> close-ups. [Werner Herzog / David Attenborough] narration style. Treated
> as a profound human struggle. [Cinematic camera work].

**예시**
- "A man trying to fix a kitchen sink. Dramatic orchestral music. Slow motion. Each failure treated like a major plot point."
- "Woman at grocery store self-checkout. Thriller voice-over narration. 'Unexpected item in bagging area.' Tense atmosphere."
- "Man trying to assemble IKEA furniture. Werner Herzog narration. Treated as a profound human struggle."

**왜 먹히나**: 누구나 공감하는 짜증을 영화화. 리텐션 매우 높음.
**최적 모델**: Veo 3 (내레이션 자동), Sora 2

---

## 7. Genre Mashup (장르 충돌)

**프롬프트 공식**
> [Genre A] [story setup], but [character is incongruous with genre].
> [Genre A's signature visual style]. [Character's incongruous behavior].

**예시**
- "Film noir detective story, 1940s black and white aesthetic, but the detective is an enthusiastic golden retriever who solves crimes by being friendly."
- "Romantic wedding montage, both characters are inanimate objects—a toaster and a coffee maker."

**왜 먹히나**: 장르 컨벤션 + 캐릭터 미스매치의 이중 충돌.

---

## 8. Split Screen Transformation (분할 화면 변신)

**프롬프트 공식**
> Split screen transformation, before and after. Dramatic lighting change
> from [mood A] to [mood B]. Subject centered. Smooth morph transition.
> [Aesthetic]. Trending [platform] style.

**예시**
- "Split screen transformation, before and after, dim warm bedroom to vibrant neon city, smooth morph, cyberpunk aesthetic."

**왜 먹히나**: 명확한 시각 대비 + 만족스러운 reveal.

---

## 9. Hyperlapse City Lights (하이퍼랩스)

**프롬프트 공식**
> Hyperlapse [location], fast forward motion, [light element] streaks,
> camera spinning [angle], [time A] to [time B] transition in [seconds].

**예시**
- "Hyperlapse city lights, neon streaks, camera spinning 360 degrees, night to day transition in 3 seconds, viral YouTube Shorts style."

**왜 먹히나**: 즉각적 시각 임팩트, 숏폼 페이싱에 완벽.

---

## 10. Seamless Loop (무한 반복 ASMR)

**프롬프트 공식**
> Seamless loop animation, [pastel/gradient] background, [floating objects],
> dreamy aesthetic, slow motion physics, satisfying ASMR visual,
> Instagram Reels trending style.

**왜 먹히나**: 무한 재생 → 알고리즘 친화. ASMR 트리거.

---

## 11. Baby Dance (2026의 알고리즘 골든티켓)

**과장된 모션 + 감정적 표정 + 초단축 루프**의 조합. 모든 숏폼 알고리즘에서
완벽에 가까운 리텐션 메트릭.

**프롬프트 공식**
> [Baby/toddler/animal subject] dancing energetically to [music style],
> exaggerated facial expressions, ultra-short 3-5 second loop, [setting],
> bright cheerful lighting.

---

## 12. Product Cinematic Ad (럭셔리 광고 톤)

**프롬프트 공식**
> Cinematic luxury [product category] advertisement. [Product description]
> on [premium surface]. Begin with extreme macro close-up of [detail], then
> slow dolly-out revealing full product. Soft diffused studio lighting with
> [accent color] highlights. Shallow depth of field, ultra-realistic
> textures, elegant minimalist background.

**예시**
> "Cinematic luxury skincare advertisement featuring a transparent glass serum bottle on polished white marble. Extreme macro close-up of the brand logo, then slow dolly-out revealing the full product. Soft diffused studio lighting with subtle golden highlights. Shallow depth of field, ultra-realistic textures, elegant minimalist background."

**최적 모델**: Veo 3 (광고 광질), Mystic+Kling 조합(이미지→영상)

# PART 3 — 바이럴 프롬프트 보편 공식 5
================================================================

1. **구체성이 곧 품질** — 모호한 프롬프트는 평범한 영상을 만든다.
   - 나쁨: "a person walks" → 좋음: "35-year-old woman in beige trench coat walks briskly through neon-lit alley"
   - 절대 "person", "dog", "car"로 끝내지 말 것

2. **인지 가능한 포맷 + 부조리한 주체** = 즉각 바이럴
   - "Realistic [익숙한 다큐 포맷]" + "[비현실적 주체]"
   - 예: bodycam + sentient taco / 1980s commercial + sentient hamburger / Werner Herzog narration + IKEA furniture

3. **현실 레퍼런스로 톤 앵커링**
   - "Wes Anderson symmetry", "Apple keynote style", "Christopher Nolan IMAX scale"
   - "Werner Herzog narration", "David Attenborough documentary tone"
   - "blade runner atmosphere", "A24 horror aesthetic"

4. **감정 톤 대비**
   - 진지한 다큐 톤 × 부조리한 소재 = 코미디
   - 영화적 광질 × 일상적 행동 = 향수/감동
   - 광고 톤 × 비상품 = 풍자

5. **카메라 워크를 명시**
   - "tracking shot from the side", "slow dolly forward", "extreme macro then pull out"
   - "static handheld POV", "360 orbit", "vertigo zoom"
   - 일반적 묘사 → 시네마틱 시퀀스로 격상

# PART 5 — 한국 컨텍스트 적응 팁
================================================================

위 영어 패턴을 한국 시장에 맞게 변형할 때:

- **Bodycam Comedy** → 한국 경찰 바디캠보다 "한국 예능 자막 + 부조리 인터뷰" 톤이
  더 잘 먹힘 (예: 무한도전·런닝맨 자막 스타일)
- **Movie Mashup** → 할리우드 명장면 + K-드라마/예능 캐릭터 조합 (예: "올드보이 장도리 신,
  하지만 주인공이 라이언이다")
- **Retro** → 90년대 한국 광고 미학(VHS, 굵은 자막, 트로트 BGM)이 동일하게 강력
- **Mundane Cinematic** → "한국식 잔소리 내레이션 + 영화적 광질" 조합 (엄마 톤 = Werner
  Herzog 대체)
- **Product Cinematic** → 한국 화장품·식음료 광고는 매크로 + 슬로우 모션 + 따뜻한
  golden hour 조명이 정석
